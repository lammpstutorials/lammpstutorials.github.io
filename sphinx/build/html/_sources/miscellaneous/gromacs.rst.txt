.. gromacs-label:

Use GROMACS instead
*******************

GROMACS is another molecular dynamics package.
It is more than 10 times faster
than LAMMPS, and can be a powerful alternative, particularly for 
large systems.

I wrote a series of tutorials for GROMACS beginners, see |gromacstutorials|.
